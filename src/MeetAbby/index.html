I have worked with K-12 students, collegiate athletes, as well as adults. In addition to having a physical education background, I have coached cross country, track and field, basketball, and volleyball. As a former teacher, I know the value of education and working with individuals to create a plan that works best for each person.

“Everyone is welcome. There is no one-size fits all for fitness or sport. I am here to help you meet your specific goals in a way that is attainable for you and your lifestyle!”

I have multiple certifications from USATF and USTFCCCA and, am working on certifications with Girls Gone Strong and Precision Nutrition. I am dedicated to continuous learning to improve myself and provide my athletes with comprehensive help to achieve their goals.
